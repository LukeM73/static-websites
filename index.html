Hobbies

Gaming

Of Course! Much like most boys born in the 2000s, I was obsessed with gaming. Every weekend without fail I would rally up my cousins and we would all play Minecraft the whole weekend straight, only giving up once one of us fell asleep.

Chess

My other obsession as a child was Chess! I would spend hours reading about all the openings in the school library just to go home and still lose to my dad.

Toys

My X-box

It seems obvious that someone so obsessed with video games would have a gaming console as one of their favorite toys. This was the Xbox that would host weekend-long gaming marathons with my cousins!

Lego

Every Christmas and birthday wishlist always had Lego on it. I would spend hours rebuilding the same set and after a few rebuilds make something entirely new, like turning a city building into a rocket - the possibilities were endless! BUT DON'T STAND ON IT!
